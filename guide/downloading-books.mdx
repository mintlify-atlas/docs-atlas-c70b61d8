---
title: Downloading books
description: Learn how to download and save books from supported portals using ReUCM
---

ReUCM allows you to download books from various supported portals and save them in FB2 format on your device.

## Finding books

There are multiple ways to access books in ReUCM:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter a book URL">
    On the home page, paste a book URL from a supported portal into the link input field and tap **Go**. The app will automatically detect the portal and navigate to the book page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse portals">
    Use the built-in browser to navigate through supported portals. When you open a book page, the app will automatically detect it and switch to the book download interface.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access recent books">
    View your recently accessed books in the "Recent" section on the home page for quick access to books you've previously viewed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
You can also share book links directly to ReUCM from your browser using Android's share menu.
</Tip>

## Downloading a book

Once you've navigated to a book page, you'll see the book's metadata including title, authors, series information, and annotation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check authorization status">
    If the book requires authorization and you're not logged in, you'll see an alert indicating you need to authorize. Configure your portal credentials in [settings](/guide/settings) first.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start the download">
    Tap the **Download** button to begin downloading the book. The app will:
    - Fetch all book chapters from the portal
    - Convert the content to FB2 format
    - Display a progress indicator during the conversion process
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for completion">
    The download progress bar will show the current stage:
    - Downloading chapters
    - Converting to FB2 format
    - Processing completed
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
You cannot navigate away from the book page while a download is in progress. The back button will be disabled until the download completes.
</Note>

## Saving books

After downloading, you have two options for managing your book:

### Share the book

Tap the **Share** button to share the downloaded FB2 file with other apps on your device. The share dialog includes:

- Book title and authors
- Series information (if applicable)
- Last downloaded chapter title
- The FB2 file attachment

### Save to device

Tap the **Save** button to save the book to your device:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Grant storage permission">
    If this is your first time saving, you'll need to grant storage permissions to the app.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose save location">
    The save behavior depends on your [save settings](/guide/settings#save-settings):
    
    - **With save directory configured**: The book is automatically saved to your configured directory using your filename template
    - **Without save directory**: A file picker dialog appears, allowing you to choose where to save the book
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    A success message appears when the book is saved successfully.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
The filename will automatically sanitize any invalid characters (< > : " / \ | ? *) from the book title and series name.
</Warning>

## Book information displayed

The book page shows comprehensive metadata:

- **Cover image**: Visual representation of the book
- **Title**: Full book title
- **Authors**: All listed authors
- **Series information**: Series name and number (if part of a series)
- **Annotation**: Book description or summary
- **Download progress**: Real-time progress during download and conversion

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues while downloading:

- **Download fails**: Check your internet connection and ensure the portal is accessible
- **Authorization required**: Configure your credentials in settings for the specific portal
- **Conversion errors**: Try downloading the book again or refresh the book page
- **Save fails**: Verify you've granted storage permissions and have sufficient storage space

<Tip>
Pull down on the book page to refresh and reload the book metadata if information appears outdated.
</Tip>
